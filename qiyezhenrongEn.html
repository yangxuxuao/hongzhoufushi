Enterprise

Hongzhou Clothing Materials Co., Ltd, is a specialized manufacturer of various kinds of woven and non woven fusible interlinings. Located in Nanjing, the provincial capital of Jiangsu, China, we enjoy convenient transportation and geographical advantage.

Hongzhou Clothing Materials Co., Ltd, has the most advanced thermal-bond nonwoven facilities, coating facilities and water jet looms. Using the best materials, it produces on the most advanced double dot facilities quality interlinings for different kinds of fabrics, including cotton, woof, linen, silk and polyester in line with international standards.

All the products has passed Oeko-Tex Standard 100 and ISO14001:2004 international certification. With a history of 33 years, Hongzhou Clothing Materials Co., Ltd now possess X workers and X cadres. The professional team ensures perfect pre-sales and after-sales technical service.
